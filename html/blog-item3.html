Summer Fridays Jet Lag Mask

Originally created to counter a travel-weary complexion, this mask is a real multi-tasker. Use and remove as usual or, for dry skin, apply generously – and after 10 minutes, massage any excess into your skin. It also makes an excellent night cream and, in a fine layer, a great day cream. But best of all is the incredible sink-in-and-disappear texture. Unlike many masks, it doesn’t leave skin tacky – just plump, healthy and rested.
